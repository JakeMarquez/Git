Instructions

  • Swap Rolls!
    To swap two rolls, simply click on two of your Ability Score numbers. You will know you have selected one when it begins to blink.
  • Know Your Strengths!
    The +1 and +2 counters represent the bonuses you recieve from your race. ({{controller.title}})
  • Name Your Character!
    Think of an original name to give your adventurer, but stay away from special characters and obscene language.
  • Ability Scores?
    If you would like information on Ability Scores, click an Ability Scores corresponding tag; ex: "STR".
